Air Fryer Frozen Vegetables

Crispy-tender frozen vegetables made in the air fryer in 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 bag (12 oz) frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or avocado oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan, lemon zest, or red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, toss frozen vegetables with oil and seasonings until evenly coated.
  3. Place vegetables in air fryer basket in a single layer.
  4. Air fry 8 minutes, shaking basket halfway through.
  5. Check for desired crispness. Add 1–2 minutes if needed.
  6. Finish with optional Parmesan or a squeeze of lemon before serving.

Substitutions

  • Oil-free: Skip oil and lightly spray with water, or broil with parchment using air fryer racks.
  • Fresh vegetables: Use broccoli, cauliflower, and carrot slices cut into 1/2-inch pieces.
  • Seasonal swap: Try curry powder, smoked paprika, or Italian seasoning for variety.
  • Higher protein: Toss in frozen edamame or chickpeas in the last 3 minutes.
