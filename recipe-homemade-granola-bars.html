Easy Homemade Granola Bars

Chewy, no-bake granola bars made with pantry staples.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper.
  2. Stir oats and salt in a large mixing bowl.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, warm honey and peanut butter until smooth; stir in vanilla.
  4. Pour wet mixture over oats. Fold until every oat is coated.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips and cranberries.
  6. Press mixture firmly and evenly into the prepared pan.
  7. Chill 30 minutes, then lift out and cut into 12 bars. Store in an airtight container.

Substitutions

  • Gluten-free: Use certified gluten-free oats.
  • Vegan: Swap honey for maple syrup; use vegan chocolate chips.
  • Nut-free: Use sunflower seed butter instead of peanut butter.
  • Refined-sugar-free: Omit chocolate chips or swap for cacao nibs.
