20-Minute Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

A bright, garlicky shrimp pasta ready in one pan in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti or linguine
  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil, for cooking

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in salted boiling water according to package directions. Reserve 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. Pat shrimp dry, season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; sauté 1 minute.
  4. Add shrimp in a single layer; cook 2 minutes per side until pink. Remove and set aside.
  5. Add drained pasta to skillet with a splash of pasta water. Toss, adding more water as needed.
  6. Return shrimp to skillet. Stir in lemon juice, lemon zest, and Parmesan.
  7. Finish with parsley; adjust seasoning.

Substitutions

  • Gluten-free: Use gluten-free pasta or zucchini noodles.
  • Dairy-free: Swap butter for vegan butter and omit Parmesan or use dairy-free Parmesan.
  • Shellfish allergy: Replace shrimp with diced chicken breast or firm tofu.
  • Lower FODMAP: Use garlic-infused oil instead of fresh garlic and omit wheat-based pasta.
