Creamy Tuscan Chicken Skillet

A restaurant-worthy one-skillet chicken dinner ready in about 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Steps

  1. Pat chicken dry and season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken 6–8 minutes per side until cooked through. Remove and tent.
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Add garlic; sauté 30 seconds.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Stir in sun-dried tomatoes.
  5. Add spinach and stir until wilted. Stir in Parmesan until melted.
  6. Return chicken to skillet; spoon sauce over the top. Garnish with basil.

Substitutions

  • Dairy-free: Use coconut cream and dairy-free Parmesan.
  • Lower-carb: Serve over cauliflower rice instead of pasta.
  • Gluten-free: Naturally gluten-free; ensure seasoning blend is gluten-free.
  • Lighter: Use chicken thighs or swap heavy cream for half-and-half.
