One-Pot Creamy Garlic Chicken

Time: 30 minutes  |  Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add chicken; season with salt and pepper; brown on all sides.
  4. Pour in cream and milk. Simmer 8–10 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  5. Finish with lemon juice, salt, pepper, and parsley. Serve with pasta or rice.

Substitutions

  • Gluten-free: Use gluten-free stock or finish with a cornstarch slurry instead of flour.
  • Dairy-free: Swap cream and milk for oat cream + oat milk; works well with garlic.
  • Extra flavor: Stir in 1 tbsp grated Parmesan at the end if you eat dairy.
